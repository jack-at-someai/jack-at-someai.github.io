SomeAI Research Lab

Infrastructure for observable reality

Abstract

This is the working surface of a research lab designed to build Charlotte — a graph-native infrastructure where everything is encoded as five primitives (Node, Edge, Metric, Signal, Protocol) and time is the primary axis of truth. Charlotte observes reality as it unfolds: identities emit signals on measurable dimensions, relationships form edges, and protocols forecast what should happen next. One substrate. Every vertical. Observable reality as a service.

The lab is organized in three layers. Layer 1 is the research hull — scientific disciplines that define what the lab studies and form the convex hull of Charlotte's intellectual foundation. Layer 2 is adversarial games — interactive complexity ladders that stress-test optimization, foresight distance, and state management. Layer 3 is the people web — advisors, mentors, engineers, and the competitive landscape that guides the lab's evolution.
